Profile

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the RI2C team of the LITIS laboratory at the Université du Havre in France.

I recently obtained my PhD from the Networks and Distributed Computing group at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Liverpool.
I finished my undergraduate and postgraduate degrees at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications of the University of Athens.

My academic interests span algorithms, complexity and their applications, mostly regarding optimization problems.
Recent work includes pursuit-evasion combinatorial games, search problems in distributed algorithms, and dynamic networks.

For any queries, feel free to contact me via e-mail.
