Profile

I am currently a Senior Research Engineer at the Huawei Paris Research Center.

I obtained my PhD in 2018 at the Department of Computer Science, University of Liverpool.

My academic interests span Algorithm Design, Combinatorial Optimization and Computational Complexity.

For any queries, feel free to contact me via e-mail.
